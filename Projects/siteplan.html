Portfolio Website Site Plan

Kinyera Alvine - Web & Programming Portfolio

Site Name

Kinyera Alvine - Web & Programming Portfolio

Domain Name: kinyeraalvine.dev

Alternative: kinyera-portfolio.com

Reason for Selection: I chose this name because it's a personal portfolio that directly represents my professional identity. Using my name creates a personal brand and makes it easy for potential clients and employers to find and remember my work. The addition of "Web & Programming" clearly indicates my specialization and expertise areas.

Site Purpose

This professional portfolio website serves multiple purposes for career development and client acquisition:

  • Showcase Technical Expertise: Display my programming skills, web development projects, and technical competencies
  • Project Portfolio: Present detailed case studies of completed projects with code samples and live demonstrations
  • Professional Services: Clearly communicate availability for full-time, part-time, and freelance opportunities
  • Contact & Networking: Provide easy ways for potential clients and employers to reach out and connect
  • Professional Credibility: Establish trust through testimonials, certifications, and professional experience

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Freelance Client

"I need a website for my small business. Can I see examples of your previous web development projects and understand what services you offer? What's your process and pricing structure?"

Scenario 2: Hiring Manager

"We're looking for a full-time developer. Can I view your resume, see your technical skills, and understand your professional background? Do you have experience with our tech stack?"

Scenario 3: Technical Recruiter

"I'm sourcing candidates for multiple positions. What programming languages do you specialize in, and can I quickly assess your skill level through your project portfolio?"

Typography

Lato (Primary Font)

Usage: Main headings, titles, and important text

Example Heading: Full-Stack Developer

This font provides excellent readability for professional content and maintains a modern, clean appearance.

Barlow (Secondary Font)

Usage: Body text, navigation, headers and footers

Barlow offers great versatility for interface elements and body text. It's designed for excellent readability across all device sizes while maintaining a professional appearance.

Typography Hierarchy

Section Headings (Lato, 2rem)

Subsection Titles (Lato, 1.5rem)

Body text and navigation (Barlow, 1rem)
